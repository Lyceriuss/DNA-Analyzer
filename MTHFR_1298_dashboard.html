MTHFR Insight: MOOD METHYLATOR

Variant Identity: rs1801131 | Systems: Nervous System, Methylation Cycle

Folate Input

Requires B9 Substrate

MTHFR Normal Activity

BH4 Recycling

Serotonin & Dopamine Synthesis

Risk: Lower BH4 recycling. Associated with Mood/Anxiety impacts.

While the C677T variant affects the 'Methylation' tail (Homocysteine), the A1298C variant (rs1801131) affects the regulatory domain. It is less thermolabile but still reduces activity. Crucially, its impact is often more neurological. It affects the recycling of BH4 (Tetrahydrobiopterin), a molecule needed to make Serotonin and Dopamine. Carriers may have normal homocysteine but struggle with mood, anxiety, or chronic pain due to low neurotransmitters.
